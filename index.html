about

Welcome! I'm Sukorno, a physics student, freelance video editor and founder of Guavacado Films. Through 8+ years of editing experience, I've enjoyed working on a variety of projects, from simple short-form TikToks to hour-long animated podcasts. I started out 10 years ago, editing videos for my own channel. Over the years, my skills improved and I fell in love with the editing world, starting a series of tutorials teaching others how to edit videos. My first clients were content creators who reached out after finding my videos, asking me to make their videos look like mine. Many years and clients later, I have created Guavacado Films out of a desire to formally establish my brand. Whether you're a content creator, a business owner, or someone with a unique story, I guarantee a smooth, fun process to bring your ideas onto the screen!
