about

Guavacado Films is an up-and-coming video production business, specialising in filming and editing services for a variety of event types. We edit your content into any format required; this includes YouTube, Facebook, Instagram, TikTok and more! We also offer a consultancy service to explore the best solutions for your brand to flourish through video marketing - this is for you if you're looking to take your brand to the next level but you're not sure where to start.


We are based in West London, and able to travel anywhere within the UK. We use equipment sourced by verified professionals, bringing you the quality your brand deserves.

This is the service you can expect when you choose to work with us:

CONCEPTUALISATION

You give us the brief, we give you the plan of action. We'll outline a rough proposal involving equipment, logistics, locations and more. Whether a simple editing job or a full-scale documentary shoot, don't be afraid to delve into the details!

PRODUCTION

We begin the main event! From voice-overs to b-roll, and anything inbetween, we capture every moment you need to efficiently communicate your story.

POST-PRODUCTION

This is when your piece will be polished up in our in-house editing suite. This includes colour grading, transitions, music, VFX and more.

FINALISATION

Once a first draft is prepared, we send it over. This is your time to give us feedback and fine-tune the content to exactly what you have in mind. After implementing any changes, your video is ready to be published!
